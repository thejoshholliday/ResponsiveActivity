This 2018 table top model FE-20 electric fan from Intertek corporation has a black rim and four grey blades. It sits on a circular black base on the bottom accompanied by a grey switch with 3 speed settings located at the top. There are also 11 concentric grey metal rings on both sides of the fan to "cage" the motorized fan. There is a silver-gray grip handle located next to the speed switch. There is an outline of silver alongside the black rim where nails are positioned to unify the design object. The power plug is located on the back where the fan is mounted.
